Security & Infrastructure

Last reviewed: March 13, 2026

1. Security Philosophy

Beaverly is designed around platform safety, automation integrity, and user privacy. Our goal is to keep automation reliable, keep access appropriately controlled, and keep sensitive information protected.

2. Non-Custodial Architecture

Beaverly does not hold, manage, or custody user funds. Chilla operates only through user-authorized connections to third-party broker environments, and capital remains with the broker at all times.

3. Data Protection

We use encrypted connections, protected account and authorization flows, restricted internal access, and safeguards around the personal data required to deliver the service. User-facing activity views are also intentionally sanitized so they stay understandable.

4. Authentication & Account Security

Beaverly applies secure sign-in, session protection, verification steps where required, and protections against repeated failed access attempts or suspicious behavior. Connected broker authorizations are handled as protected credentials and are limited to the scope required for user-approved automation.

Users are responsible for keeping their email account, device access, passwords, and connected broker credentials safe, and for reviewing linked environments regularly.

5. Infrastructure Security

We use layered production safeguards, hardened request handling, restricted administrative pathways, and ongoing operational monitoring to protect platform availability and integrity. We also review and remediate reported issues.

6. Automation Integrity

Chilla executes only within the instructions, authorization scope, and connected environment selected by the user. Before automation proceeds, the platform applies validation, compatibility, guardrail, and comfort-boundary checks.

Capacity and fair-use controls are also used to keep service stable for all users. When a boundary is reached, the platform is designed to pause, defer, or skip rather than act outside approved limits.

7. Continuous Monitoring

Beaverly monitors platform health, connection health, warning signals, unusual activity patterns, and overall service performance. These signals help us investigate abnormal conditions, respond to degraded service, and maintain operational reliability.

8. Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact: support@beaverlyai.com

We review reports responsibly and work to resolve verified issues as quickly as practical.

9. Privacy Commitment

Beaverly is committed to protecting user data and using it only as needed to provide, secure, and improve the service. For more information, please review:

  • Privacy Policy
  • Terms of Service
  • Risk Disclosure

10. Final Statement

Beaverly is designed as a secure automation infrastructure platform. We do not custody funds, provide financial advice, or act on our own behalf in the market. Our role is to operate the technology environment where your automation instructions run.
