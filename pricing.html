Automation capacity

How automation capacity works

Every day, Chilla has a capacity for how much automation work it can run on your behalf. Think of it as the total automation fuel available to your account in a 24-hour window.

It resets daily.

Capacity refreshes each day, so you're never penalised for yesterday's activity.

Different behaviors use different amounts.

Some automation actions are lighter; others — particularly when monitoring many playgrounds simultaneously — use more capacity. Chilla manages this automatically within your configured instructions.

Guardrails protect system reliability.

Paid plans are designed to feel roomy in normal use. The guardrails exist to keep Chilla reliable and consistent for everyone — not to restrict everyday automation.

Questions

Good questions, calm answers.

What does "unlimited with guardrails" mean?

It means your automation isn't arbitrarily capped — in normal use, you'll rarely if ever notice a limit. The guardrails are system-level safeguards that protect reliability for all Chilla users, not restrictions on everyday automation. They're designed to be invisible unless something unusual is happening.

Can I change plans later?

Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade anytime from inside Chilla. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you'd like to talk through a mid-cycle change, reach out to the team at hello@beaverlyai.com.

Does Chilla hold my funds?

No — never. Your funds remain in your own environment account at all times. Beaverly doesn't hold, manage, or have access to your capital. We provide automation infrastructure that follows your instructions. You withdraw, deposit, and manage your money directly through your own environment.

What is automation capacity?

It's the total amount of automation work Chilla can run on your behalf in a single day. Think of it like daily automation fuel — it powers the monitoring, instruction processing, and behavior execution happening in the background. It resets every 24 hours, and you can always see where you stand inside Chilla.

Why do I see values like 12,000× in-app?

The × symbol represents individual automation cycles — each one a discrete piece of work Chilla completes within your instructions. 12,000× means 12,000 automation runs available that day. Most users with active behaviors find their daily allocation more than comfortable.

Do different behaviors use capacity differently?

Yes. Lighter monitoring actions use less capacity; more complex behaviors — particularly those watching many playgrounds simultaneously — use a bit more. Chilla manages this automatically. Your usage panel always shows how much you've used and how much remains, so there's no guesswork.

What counts as an environment connection?

An environment connection is a link between Chilla and a single account. If you have one account, you need one connection. If you want Chilla to automate across multiple accounts — different environments, regions, or account types — each one counts as a separate environment connection.

Can I test before enabling live automation?

Yes — that's exactly what Chill Architect is for. It's free, and it lets you configure behaviors, set comfort levels, and see precisely how your instructions are structured before a single live automation runs. When you're ready, upgrading to a paid plan activates everything you've already set up.

Which plan if I want more than one environment?

Deep Chill supports up to 3 environment connections — a comfortable starting point for multi-environment automation. If you need more, Peak Chill offers unlimited connections (with guardrails), and Enterprise is available for institutional-scale requirements.

Do all plans include Flow?

Yes. Flow — your real-time decision log — is included across all plans, including the free Chill Architect tier. Transparency is core to how Chilla works. You'll always be able to see what Chilla is doing and why, within your instructions.

Does Chilla guarantee outcomes?

No — and we'll never claim otherwise. Chilla is disciplined automation infrastructure, not a financial advisor or performance engine. All markets involve uncertainty. What Chilla offers is consistency, transparency, and the removal of emotional or time-based friction from your participation. Outcomes depend on market conditions and the behaviors you configure.

Is yearly billing locked in?

Yearly plans are billed as a single annual payment and include a meaningful saving compared to monthly billing. If your circumstances change, reach out to the team at hello@beaverlyai.com — we're here to help.
