Automation capacity

How automation capacity works

Every day, Chilla has a capacity for how much automation work it can run on your behalf. Think of it as the total automation fuel available to your account in a 24-hour window.

It resets daily.

Capacity refreshes each day, so you're never penalised for yesterday's activity.

Different behaviors use different amounts.

Some automation actions are lighter; others — particularly when monitoring many playgrounds simultaneously — use more capacity. Chilla manages this automatically within your configured instructions.

Guardrails protect system reliability.

Paid plans are designed to feel roomy in normal use. The guardrails exist to keep Chilla reliable and consistent for everyone — not to restrict everyday automation.
