Automation Guardrails & Usage

Effective Date: June 28, 2025

1. Purpose of Guardrails

Beaverly® is designed to enable users participate in global markets through controlled automation, without becoming traders. To achieve this safely and reliably, all automation on the Chilla™ platform operates within defined guardrails.

These guardrails exist to protect users, maintain platform stability, and ensure automation behaves exactly as instructed — and never beyond it.

2. What “Unlimited Automation (with Guardrails)” Means

When Beaverly refers to “unlimited automation”, this means:

  • No per-behaviour fees
  • No per-playground (market/symbol) charges
  • No limits on the number of active instructions you can configure

However, all automation is subject to usage guardrails to prevent misuse, unintended over-consumption of resources, or unsafe execution patterns. These guardrails do not override user instructions — they enforce them.

3. Automation Capacity & Fair Usage

Each subscription tier includes a defined daily automation capacity. This capacity represents the amount of computational execution available to process your active instructions across environments.

Automation capacity is not tied to profit, trade volume, or account size. It exists solely to ensure consistent, reliable service for all users.

  • Chill Mode: Standard automation capacity suitable for continuous participation
  • Deep Chill: Higher automation capacity for broader, more active configurations
  • Peak Chill: Maximum automation capacity for complex, always-on usage

If capacity limits are reached, automation pauses gracefully until capacity resets. No instructions are altered, skipped, or modified.

4. User-Defined Risk Controls (Omega Shield)

Chilla™ includes optional user-controlled safety mechanisms such as Omega Shield.

Omega Shield allows users to define a monthly risk budget. When the configured budget is exhausted:

  • Automation may halt automatically for the remainder of the period
  • No further execution may occur until the next cycle or manual adjustment

Omega Shield is a technical enforcement tool, not financial advice, and operates strictly based on user-defined parameters.

5. Execution Transparency (Flow)

All active automation produces a human-readable execution flow. This includes:

  • What action occurred
  • When it occurred
  • Where it was executed
  • Why it occurred based on your instructions

Flow explanations are designed for clarity and understanding, not trading jargon or professional terminology.

6. What Guardrails Do Not Do

  • They do not make decisions for you
  • They do not change your instructions
  • They do not provide investment advice
  • They do not manage or custody funds

Beaverly remains a non-custodial, non-advisory, and non-discretionary technology provider at all times.

7. Temporary Restrictions & Service Protection

In rare cases, Beaverly may temporarily restrict automation if:

  • Usage patterns appear abusive or unintended
  • System integrity or platform stability is at risk
  • Regulatory or legal obligations require intervention

When this occurs, users are notified through the dashboard or email. If a restriction is determined to be applied in error, normal access is restored.

8. Transparency Commitment

Beaverly is committed to clear communication around system behavior, limits, and safeguards. Our goal is to help users participate with confidence — not confusion.
