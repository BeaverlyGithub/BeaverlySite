How Chilla Works

A guided setup. A calm experience.

Getting started is deliberate by design. Every step requires your explicit approval before anything runs.

01

Connect your environment

Authorize your broker account through a secure connection flow. Chilla supports multiple accounts under a connected environment — you choose which account is active for automation. Your funds remain with your broker throughout.

Secure broker authorization flow
Multi-account support under one environment
Automation toggle per account
Broker and account health visible on dashboard
02

Choose behaviors and set your comfort

Browse the behavior catalog. Filter by compatibility and select what fits your approach. Set your per-position comfort level. Optionally cap monthly risk. Chilla shows you the risk implications before anything is activated.

Behavior catalog with filtering and metadata
Per-position comfort input
Optional monthly risk budget cap
Risk implication preview before activation
03

Review, consent, and activate

Before activation, Chilla surfaces the consent document, captures your explicit authorization, and validates your configuration. Instructions are version-tracked from the moment they go live. You can run multiple instructions concurrently, each isolated from the others.

Consent document retrieval and acceptance capture
Version-tracked instruction activation
Multiple concurrent instructions supported
Full pre-activation validation gate
04

Monitor, adjust, or pause — anytime

Once active, your dashboard shows live instruction status, risk usage, and account readiness. The Flow feed surfaces every meaningful system event in plain language. Update your instructions, revoke them with confirmation, or disconnect accounts without losing your environment setup.
