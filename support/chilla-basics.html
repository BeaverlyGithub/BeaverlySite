Where to find this

After login, you typically land on the main dashboard. From there, main navigation gives access to behaviors, instructions, Flow, usage, environments, and account controls.

Verification note: Exact tab names and icon labels can change between releases. Use the app labels in your account as source of truth for final click paths.

What you can do here

  • Check what is active and what is paused.
  • Open a behavior and assign it to a playground.
  • Set comfort controls before activation.
  • Track automation capacity and remaining room for the day.
  • Review Flow events if activity looks different than expected.
  • Change plan tier when your usage profile changes.

Common questions

Is every area actionable?

No. Some areas are informational (status, history, usage), while others are actionable (connect, activate, pause, revoke, upgrade).

Can I run without Flow?

Flow transparency is a core part of Chilla. Use it as your first check when reviewing activity.

Does Chilla act outside selected environment scope?

No. Chilla automation is account-scoped to user-authorized environment connections.
