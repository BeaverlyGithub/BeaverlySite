Where to find this

After login, Chilla opens `dashboard.html`. Open the sidebar from the top-left menu icon. The sidebar is the entry point for Connect Chilla, Settings, Usage, and support/policy pages.

What each dashboard card does

CardWhat you will seeWhat you can do
Not connected yet / status cardConnection state and status text.Use account switch and connect controls.
InstructionsStatus badge and instruction summary.Click settings icon to open Your Instructions.
FlowRecent events, summary counts, and action buttons.Filter events, replay logs, expand, and download JSON logs.
Active PlaygroundsCurrent tracked playgrounds.Review active playground coverage.

What you can do from this area

  • Connect or disconnect environments.
  • Create instructions from Tap to create instructions or open Your Instructions for management.
  • Open Usage & Capacity from sidebar Usage.
  • Go to Upgrade from the sidebar tier panel.
  • Open support channels from sidebar Contact and FAQ.

Common questions

Is every area actionable?

No. Some areas are informational (status, history, usage), while others are actionable (connect, activate, pause, revoke, upgrade).

Can I run without Flow?

Flow transparency is a core part of Chilla. Use it as your first check when reviewing activity.

Does Chilla act outside selected environment scope?

No. Chilla automation is account-scoped to user-authorized environment connections.
