Billing basics

  • Upgrade page includes Monthly and Annual billing toggle states.
  • Usage page includes direct upgrade CTAs when capacity is high or reached.
  • Billing timing, proration, and refund policy are handled by support; include billing timestamps and plan name in requests.

Usage page walk-through

  • Used Today: percentage indicator in the circular progress block.
  • Automation Capacity Used: used value with backend-provided display formatting.
  • Capacity Available: remaining value for the same day.
  • Daily Available: full daily limit for the account tier.
  • x suffix: automation capacity units, not financial outcome units.
  • Your Plan Includes: Connected Environments, Active Playgrounds, Active Behaviours.

Plans and billing FAQ

Can I start free and upgrade later?

Yes. Chill Architect supports setup and understanding before moving to live automation tiers.

Where do I ask a billing question?

Use the support contact path and include account email, plan name, date, and issue summary.

Does a higher tier guarantee outcomes?

No. Tiers increase automation room and support scope, not outcome certainty.
