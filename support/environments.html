Where to find this

Environment connection controls are available in setup and account management areas. You can typically connect, verify, and select an active environment from these sections.

Verification note: Supported provider list and exact selector layout should be validated against production UI at publish time.

What you can do here

  • Connect a new environment.
  • Verify connection status and account readiness.
  • Choose which connected environment is active.
  • Disconnect an account without deleting other setup context.

Before you connect

  • Have access credentials for the target environment account.
  • Confirm you are using the correct account context.
  • Complete any required account-level verification steps.
  • Check plan limits for number of environment connections.

Connect an environment

  1. Open the environment connection screen.
  2. Choose provider and account details.
  3. Authorize the connection.
  4. Wait for verification. Status should move to connected or ready.
  5. Set active environment. Confirm instruction scope points to the expected account.

Connection states and what they mean

StateMeaningAction
Not connectedNo active environment link yet.Start connection flow.
PreparingSystem is validating connection readiness.Wait briefly; then troubleshoot if it stalls.
ConnectedEnvironment is linked and available.Assign instruction scope and monitor Flow.
FailedConnection did not complete validation.Review credentials and retry.
DisconnectedLink removed or expired.Reconnect and confirm active selection.

Environment FAQ

Can I connect multiple environments?

Yes, based on your current plan tier. See plans and usage docs for connection allowances.

How do I know which environment is active?

Check instruction context and environment status cards before activation.

Does Beaverly hold my funds after connection?

No. Funds remain in your own environment account.
