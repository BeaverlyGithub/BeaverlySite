What you can do here

  • Select compatible playgrounds.
  • Assign behavior choices into instruction setup.

How to compare behaviors safely

  • Use behavior descriptions, not outcome promises.
  • Look for fit with your comfort boundaries and participation style.
  • Review temperament history context where available.
  • Confirm environment and playground compatibility before activation.
