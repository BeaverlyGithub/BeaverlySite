Support FAQ

Does Beaverly hold my funds?

No. Funds remain in your own connected environment account. Beaverly provides instruction-driven automation infrastructure only.

Does Chilla give financial advice?

No. Chilla automates within your instructions. It is not a financial advisor, brokerage, or recommendation engine.

Why are these docs explicit about app locations?

Support content is task-first. Each app-area article includes "Where to find this" and "What you can do here" to reduce confusion.
