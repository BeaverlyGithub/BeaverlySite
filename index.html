Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chilla AI?

Chilla™ is an AI-powered execution system that connects to your MetaTrader 5 account and trades autonomously based on market analysis and your risk parameters.

Do I need a VPS?

A VPS ensures 24/7 operation. Our Standard and Premium plans include VPS hosting, or you can use your own with the Basic plan.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Your funds remain in your broker account. Beaverly never has custody of your money—we only provide the execution software.

What returns can I expect?

Returns depend on market conditions and your risk settings. Chilla™ focuses on consistent, intelligent execution rather than guaranteeing specific returns.

Can I test Chilla before paying?

Yes. You can start with a demo account to experience full functionality before deploying on live markets.

How do I renew my license?

We'll send renewal reminders before expiration. You can renew manually through your account dashboard.
