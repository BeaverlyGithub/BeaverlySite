What if I don't have a broker account?

+

If you are new to this, register with a regulated broker in your jurisdiction e.g Deriv, Exness. Right now, Chilla can connect to any regulated broker in the world, but our database can only accommodate MT5 credentials. We are working on onboarding credentials beyond MT5 users.

Where can I use Chilla?

+

You can chill with Chilla on any broker But our onboarding credentials only supports MT5 users. We’re unlocking more credential onboarding soon. Our goal? Wherever your money moves, Chilla moves with you. Effortlessly.

Can I withdraw funds anytime?

+

Yes, absolutely. Chilla doesn’t hold anything. Your money stays in your broker account and you have full control at all times. You can chill without feeling locked in.

Do I need to know trading or do anything?

+

Not even a little. You just chill. Chilla was built to handle the complexity so you don’t have to.

Is Chilla a bot or investment platform?

+

Chilla is your wealth co-pilot that helps you live a chilled life while doing the heavy lifting happily—not an investment platform. We never hold your money. You maintain full control of your capital through your own broker account.

Is my money safe?

+

Yes. Your funds remain in your regulated broker account. Beaverly never has custody of your money—we only provide your wealth co-pilot.

What returns can I expect?
