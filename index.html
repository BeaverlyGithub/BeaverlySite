FAQ

Questions before you chill.

Chilla is not a bank, not an investment service, not a brokerage. Your money stays with you — always.

What is Chilla?

Chilla™ is Beaverly's user-facing automation product. You connect your environment, configure instructions, set your comfort boundaries, and Chilla automates in the background — strictly within the scope you approved. Flow keeps you informed of every action in plain language.

What is Beaverly?

Beaverly is building instruction-based automation infrastructure for structured, non-custodial market participation. Chilla is the first product on that infrastructure. M-II is the automation layer that powers everything underneath it.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Your funds remain in your regulated broker account at all times. Beaverly never holds or has custody of your money. We only provide the automation infrastructure — you retain full access to your account throughout.

Can I pause or withdraw anytime?

Absolutely. You can pause automation or revoke any instruction from your dashboard at any moment. Your funds are in your broker account — your ability to withdraw is entirely independent of Chilla.

How do I get started?

Register on Chilla, connect your environment, choose a behavior, set your comfort level, review comfort implications, give your authorization, and watch Flow. The free Chill Architect plan lets you explore the full setup before running any live automation.

Do I need trading knowledge?

You don't need to be a trader, but you should understand that markets involve uncertainty and make your own informed instruction choices. Chilla is designed for people who want structured participation — not a black box they hand decisions to.

Does Chilla guarantee returns?

No. Markets involve uncertainty and outcomes vary. Chilla automates within your instructions and boundaries — it makes no promises about results. It keeps you informed through Flow and enforces the safety guardrails you define.

What is Flow?

Flow is Chilla's real-time transparency feed. Every meaningful action the system takes — entries, exits, skipped actions, warnings — appears in plain language, in order. Filter, replay, and export your full activity history at any time.
