Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chilla AI?

+

Chilla™ is an AI-powered execution system that connects to your MetaTrader 5 account and trades autonomously based on market analysis and your risk parameters.

How do I get access after payment?

+

You'll receive a unique Chilla license key tied to your MT5 account + install guide within 24 hours of payment confirmation.

Do I need my own VPS?

+

Only for the Basic plan. Our Standard and Premium plans include VPS hosting. You can also use your own VPS with any plan.

Can I withdraw funds anytime?

+

Yes, absolutely. We never hold your funds. Your money stays in your broker account and you have full control at all times.

What if I want a refund?

+

We offer a 5-day refund window for first-time users. This gives you time to test and ensure Chilla meets your expectations.

Is Chilla a bot or investment platform?

+

Chilla is AI execution technology—not an investment platform. We never hold your money. You maintain full control of your capital through your own broker account.

Is my money safe?

+

Yes. Your funds remain in your regulated broker account. Beaverly never has custody of your money—we only provide the execution software.

What returns can I expect?

+

Returns depend on market conditions and your risk settings. Chilla™ focuses on consistent, intelligent execution rather than guaranteeing specific returns.
